Who I Am

About Me

I'm Chris Cerney, a reporter and photo/videographer with experience creating news packages, promotional content, and advertisements for live audiences and digital platforms.

From field reporting and news packages to in-house cinema ads, short-form marketing content, and event coverage, I focus on producing clean, professional visuals with strong storytelling and fast turnaround. Whether it's covering breaking news in the field, capturing the energy of a live event, or delivering polished promotional campaigns, my goal is always the same — create content that connects with people and drives engagement.
